The Program Pathway

A Structured Learning Journey

We value talent and commitment. Our program is divided into clear, performance-based phases to support your growth from learning to direct professional impact.

Phase 1 (2 Months)

Learning & Integration

Free/Unpaid Internship

Establish your foundation. You will get familiar with our active engineering guidelines, build prototype features, and collaborate on codebase reviews with senior mentor feedback.

  • Comprehensive onboarding
  • Guided tasks & code reviews
  • Flexible remote/hybrid hours
  • Evaluation for Phase 2 at Month 2
Phase 2 (4 Months)

Feature Ownership

Paid Internship

Transition into active product contribution. Based on your Phase 1 performance, you will assume module ownership and receive a monthly stipend for delivering production features.

  • Monthly performance stipend
  • Direct engineering responsibility
  • Collaborative startup sprints
  • FTE evaluation checkpoints
Phase 3

Full-Time Transition

FTE Conversion

Join the core team. High-performing interns who complete Phase 2 will receive a direct conversion offer for a full-time engineering or growth placement within HimStack.

  • Competitive full-time salary
  • Full employee benefits
  • Rapid growth opportunities
  • Core member equity potential
About HimStack

A Modern Technology Startup

HimStack is a dynamic product and service-based technology startup founded on 3 June 2026. We build intelligent, AI-powered ERP platforms and cloud solutions tailored for schools, colleges, coaching institutes, and educational organizations.

By joining our internship cohort, you will work directly on active product systems, contribute to production codebases, and experience first-hand how an agile startup operates from the ground up.

Our Core Mission

To transform educational institutions with modern, automated, and intelligent software systems that reduce administrative friction, automate workloads, and enhance learning outcomes.
